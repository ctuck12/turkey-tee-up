# Turkey Tee Up — Demo Video Script & Storyboard

**Target length:** ~90 seconds · **Narration pace:** ~150 wpm
**How to record:** Open the live site, follow the on-screen actions in order, and read
the narration over each scene (QuickTime/Loom screen recording, or feed the VO column
into a text-to-speech tool and sync to a screen capture).

---

### SCENE 1 — Open / Welcome  (0:00–0:10)
**On screen:** App loads. Header "ABILENE TURKEY DRIVE · 5TH ANNUAL TURKEY TEE UP."
The welcome card appears with **Scorekeeper** and **Viewer** buttons.

> **VO:** "Meet the Abilene Turkey Drive Golf app — the live home for our annual Turkey
> Tee Up tournament. When you open it, just pick how you're playing today: scoring a
> team, or watching the action."

---

### SCENE 2 — Live Leaderboard  (0:10–0:30)
**On screen:** Tap **Viewer**. Leaderboard loads. Slowly scroll the standings.
Tap the **AM / PM flight** toggle to switch flights. Point out the closest-to-pin
and long-drive entries.

> **VO:** "The leaderboard updates in real time — every score posted on the course shows
> up here within seconds, no refresh needed. Flip between the morning and afternoon
> flights, and track the closest-to-pin and long-drive winners as they happen."

---

### SCENE 3 — Scorekeeper  (0:30–0:50)
**On screen:** Tap **Scorekeeper** in the nav. Enter a team code. The team's scorecard
opens. Type a couple of hole scores, show the running total updating. Tap **Submit**.

> **VO:** "Each team gets a private code. Scorekeepers log in, tap in their hole-by-hole
> scores, and watch their total update instantly. One tap to submit when the round's
> done — and it's live on the leaderboard for everyone."

---

### SCENE 4 — Admin Portal  (0:50–1:18)
**On screen:** Tap **Admin**, enter password. Walk the four tabs:
- **Teams** — show the AM/PM list, a team card, copy a team code.
- **Submitted** — show the progress view (who's in, who's still out).
- **Broadcast** — type a message, hit Send; cut to the announcement popping up for viewers.
- **Settings** — show **Manage CTP & LD Entries** with a player/team assigned.

> **VO:** "Behind the scenes, the admin portal runs the whole event. Manage teams and
> their codes, see who's submitted and who's still on the course, and send an
> announcement that pops up instantly for everyone watching. You can also set the
> closest-to-pin and long-drive winners by hand, right from settings."

---

### SCENE 5 — Donate + Close  (1:18–1:30)
**On screen:** Tap **Donate**. Show the donation page. End on the app logo / leaderboard.

> **VO:** "And because it's all for a good cause, donating is one tap away. Abilene Turkey
> Drive — the whole tournament, live in your pocket."

---

## Quick reference — features to highlight
| Feature | Where | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Real-time updates | Leaderboard | Scores appear in seconds (live SSE stream) |
| AM/PM flights | Leaderboard toggle | Two-wave tournament format |
| CTP / Long Drive | Leaderboard + Admin | Side contests tracked live |
| Team-code login | Scorekeeper | Secure, per-team score entry |
| Submit round | Scorekeeper | Locks the card, flags as done |
| Teams / Submitted / Broadcast / Settings | Admin Portal | Full event control |
| Live announcements | Admin → Broadcast | Push a popup to every viewer |
| Donate | Nav | Fundraising tie-in |
| Add to Home Screen | First launch | Installs as a full-screen app (PWA) |

## Recording tips
- Record on a **phone-shaped** window (≈390×844) — the app is mobile-first.
- Pre-load some demo scores so the leaderboard looks full.
- For the broadcast beat, have a second window open as "Viewer" to capture the popup.
- Keep cursor movements slow; let each screen breathe ~2 seconds.

# Turkey Tee Up — 30-Second Cut (Social / Text Sharing)

**Target length:** ~30 seconds · **Narration pace:** ~150 wpm
**Use for:** Group texts, Instagram/Facebook stories, a quick "here's the app" share.
Fast, punchy, one feature per beat.

### SCENE 1 — Hook  (0:00–0:06)
**On screen:** App loads — header and welcome card. Quick, snappy.

> **VO:** "The Abilene Turkey Drive tournament — live in your pocket."

### SCENE 2 — Live Leaderboard  (0:06–0:15)
**On screen:** Tap **Viewer**. Scroll the standings; tap the **AM / PM** toggle once.

> **VO:** "Watch the leaderboard update in real time — every flight, every score, the
> second it's posted."

### SCENE 3 — Scorekeeper  (0:15–0:23)
**On screen:** Tap **Scorekeeper**, enter a code, type a score, tap **Submit**.

> **VO:** "Scoring your team? Punch in your code, tap your scores, hit submit. Done."

### SCENE 4 — Close  (0:23–0:30)
**On screen:** Quick flash of the Admin broadcast popup, then end on the logo / Donate.

> **VO:** "Live announcements, full admin control, and one-tap donating — all for a good
> cause. Turkey Tee Up."

---

### Tips for the short cut
- Cut faster — ~1.5 seconds per shot, no lingering.
- Add upbeat background music; duck it under the VO.
- End on a clear call-to-action card (the URL or "Scan to play").
